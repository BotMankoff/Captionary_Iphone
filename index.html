How to Play

Goal: Match each cartoon with its correct caption.

How to Swap: Tap one caption to select it. Tap a second caption to swap their positions. Correct matches turn green!

Tips:
• Tap any cartoon to enlarge it.
• Use ROTATE to switch to a new face.
• NEW will reset all puzzles on all faces.
